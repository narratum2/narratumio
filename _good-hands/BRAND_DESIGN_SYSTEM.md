- ‚úÖ Negative space
- ‚úÖ Shallow depth of field
- ‚úÖ Leading lines
- ‚ùå Centered subjects
- ‚ùå Cluttered backgrounds

- ‚úÖ Authentic moments
- ‚úÖ Hands in action
- ‚úÖ Close-up details
- ‚úÖ Lisbon architecture
- ‚úÖ Portuguese elements (tiles, etc.)
- ‚ùå Overly posed

Vibrance: +10
